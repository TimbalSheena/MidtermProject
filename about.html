Little Feet

Hi, my name is Sheena! I am a citizen of a small town in my country, Philippines. As I am pacing on my career as a student, I confined myself to the city, away from my comfort, with a greater hope of pursuing college.

I wonder to wander. Living my life on busy streets of the city, I feel like an outcast. I have this zest in life, to throw myself into the unknown and create every uncertainties worth it. One day, the feel of the sea on my skin will linger, the forest smell, the morning light on a hiking trip, the enthralling scenes on all roads taken. One day in my life, there will be the 'day one' on all my adventure awaiting. My life is like my dream adventures. They're quiet risky and brave yet I have the claim for it, I can go to places I want and make the most happen. Creating moments and heart wrenching views feeds my hopes and devulge on the cup of coffee for my good soul. There are aspects in my life yet to wander and yet, here I am, brave to take the adventure.
